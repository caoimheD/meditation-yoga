Benefits of meditation

  • Reduces negative emotions and stress
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Helps focus on the present
  • Increases self-awareness

Benefits of yoga

  • Improves strength, balance and flexibility
  • Helps with back pain relief
  • Helps to relax, reduces anxiety
  • Benefits heart health and posture

3 easy steps for meditation

  1. Find a quiet and comfortable place
  2. Close your eyes and focus on your breath
  3. Detach and let go of thoughts
